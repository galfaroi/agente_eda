WOSET was founded by OpenROAD project members in 2018, and we&#8217;re very glad to see that the workshop continues to flourish with all the efforts of Professors Guthaus and Renau at UC Santa Cruz!</p>    <h2 class="wp-block-heading">Call for Papers</h2>    <h3 class="wp-block-heading">*** Workshop on Open Source EDA Technologies (WOSET) ***</h3>    <p><a href="https://urldefense.com/v3/__https://woset-workshop.github.io/__;!!Mih3wA!EjspXUR1VY5bNOYdd6mTWx8xmGiLlIRa15ngPde7xz3Byx6wkyMua3Fnl7r5VjSqj1HwPz9c$" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://urldefense.com/v3/__https://woset-workshop.github.io/__;!!Mih3wA!EjspXUR1VY5bNOYdd6mTWx8xmGiLlIRa15ngPde7xz3Byx6wkyMua3Fnl7r5VjSqj1HwPz9c$</a></p>    <h3 class="wp-block-heading">Virtual! No registration fee!</h3>    <p>The <strong>WOSET </strong>workshop aims to galvanize the open-source EDA movement. The<br>workshop will (virtually) bring together EDA researchers who are committed to<br>open-source principles to share their experiences and coordinate<br>efforts towards developing a reliable, fully open-source EDA flow. The<br>workshop will feature presentations that overview existing<br>or under-development open-source tools, designs and technology<br>libraries. Break-out rooms will be utilized for discussion of works-in-progress.<br>The workshop will feature a panel on the present status and<br>future challenges in open-source EDA, and how to coordinate efforts<br>and ensure quality and interoperability across open-source tools.</p>    <p><strong>Topics of interest</strong> include, but are not limited to:</p>    <ul class="wp-block-list"> <li>Overview of an existing or under-development open-source EDA tool.</li>    <li>Overview of support infrastructure (e.g. EDA databases and design benchmarks).</li>    <li>Open-source cloud-based EDA tools</li>    <li>Open-source hardware designs</li>    <li>Position statements (e.g. critical gaps, blockers/obstacles)</li> </ul>    <p><strong>Submission Information</strong></p>    <ul class="wp-block-list"> <li>All submissions must include links to open-source repositories with<br>all source code and an open-source license (BSD, GPL, Apache, etc.)</li>    <li>Please reference your open-source repository!</li>    <li>Review is single blind (anonymous reviewers).</li>    <li>Videos will be put on the WOSET site if accepted.</li>    <li>Virtual presentation for regular papers (in addition to archival video)</li>    <li>Regular Paper Submissions (4 pages + 1 page references + 15 min video + virtual presentation)</li>    <li>Work in Progress Submissions (2 page abstract + 1 page references + 10 min video + virtual zoom room)</li> </ul>    <p><strong><strong>Submission site</strong></strong>:</p>    <p><a href="https://urldefense.com/v3/https://openreview.net/group?id=WOSET-Workshop.github.io*2024;Lw!!Mih3wA!EjspXUR1VY5bNOYdd6mTWx8xmGiLlIRa15ngPde7xz3Byx6wkyMua3Fnl7r5VjSqj4MLEPuQ$">https://openreview.net/group?id=WOSET-Workshop.github.io*2024;Lw!!Mih3wA!EjspXUR1VY5bNOYdd6mTWx8xmGiLlIRa15ngPde7xz3Byx6wkyMua3Fnl7r5VjSqj4MLEPuQ$</a></p>
